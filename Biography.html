Born in the DRC but raised in South Africa , David Mbombo is a Gospel artist formed in the Endtime Message Believing Church.
While growing up although his parents, Jean Maurice and Guilaine Mondonga took him to church constantly , David never showed interest in Church activities ,
but he was more focused on outsiders what we call Worldly Pleasures or bad influence.
Between the ages of three and five David use to take a stick and use it as a guitar,
When it was time to use the stick in the kitchen to cook fufu , he always cried and could not wait for Mom to clean it again , before David could take it again and pretend it's a guitar .
David came to conscious of his gifted talent playing the guitar and singing at the ages of 19 , whilst playing and singing infront of a Vocal coach ,
who told him his got a great soothing tone , that goes well with the guitar.
The rest is history , after dedicating his life to Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior
he started singing in his local Church and eventually built the courage to start singing his own compositions .
On the 28 of February was an official release of his very first single titled God in Simplicity , which marked the begining of his Musical Career.
